My first flight flying alone

Dec 21, 2015

I was traveling for the first time on my own. I was going to Toronto and was going to be there for a few weeks. It would also be the first time I would experience snow.

Toronto

A few days in toronto and I did fall in love with the place. There is plenty of things to do and public transport is actually really good compared to areas I'm used to in the US. I will include photos below that I enjoyed taking of Toronto. Poutine tastes fucking amazing, pardon my french.

Niagara Falls

I got to experience Niagara Falls for a few days. To be honest there isn't a whole lot to do thats unique at Niagara falls but I do recommend people to come and experience the falls at least once.

Random Areas

I also took pictures of random locations that attracted my attention while in Canada. I did forget to mention above that snow is quite fun and I turned into a small kid and enjoyed doing snowball fights and such.
